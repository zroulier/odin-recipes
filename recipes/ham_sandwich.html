Ham Sandwich Recipe

Description

Pasta, originating from Italy, is a common staple in many households across the world. This simple recipe contains only a few ingredients: pasta, water, sauce, and spices for flavoring. This page will give you everything you need to cook an amazing plate of pasta tonight.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked pasta; any type
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Red pasta sauce
  • Any spices, especially black pepper

Steps

  1. Fill up a pot with water and bring to a boil
  2. Once it is boiling add the uncooked pasta
  3. Wait 7-10 minutes, depending on how soft you want your pasta; stir occassionally
  4. Straing the water out of the pot and take the pasta off the heat
  5. Add the sauce to the pot and mix
  6. Add your pasta to a bowl; top with salt and pepper
  7. Enjoy!
