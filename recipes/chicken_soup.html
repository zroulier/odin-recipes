Chicken Soup Recipe

Description

Chicken soup is a great meal to have whether you are sick, lazy to make a big meal, or anything in between! Chicken soup consists of only a few ingredients and you can cook this meal in only a few minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Black pepper
  • Chicken broth cubes

Steps

  1. Fill up a pot with water and bring to a boil
  2. Add two chicken broth cubes to the water once it begins to heat up
  3. Add in cooked pieces of chicken, carrots, and peas
  4. Cook for 5-6 minutes on medium heat
  5. Enjoy!
